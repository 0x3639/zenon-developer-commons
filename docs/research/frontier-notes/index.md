# Frontier Notes (0x01–0x10)

A numbered series of working research notes exploring the edges of the verification-first model — minimal state tracking, verifier lifecycle, bounded verification and refusal, proof availability, verification frontiers and local finality, multi-verifier consistency, application semantics, incentives under partial verification, and governance without global consensus.

These are working notes (draft-grade) but substantive; they trace the open problems that the formal specs and papers build on.
